Ophelia is a Sylph, a tiny elemental fairy-like being of the [[Elemental Plane of Air]]. She was freed from the [[Synod of Elevation]] by [[Rylai Sweetsong]] of the [[Vanguard]]. She has the statblock of [[Sylph Companion]].
