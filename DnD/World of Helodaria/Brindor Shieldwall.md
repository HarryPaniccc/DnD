Brindor is the head forge cleric in the [[Dularen Heart-Kiln]], the temple to [[Moradin]]. Copper Skin, bald, full white beard that is a bit burnt. Strong Spoken deeper hoarse voice with slight scottish accent

Brindor is a 12th-level forge cleric, and usually has prepared
- Raise dead (5th level)
- Sunbeam (6th level)
- Whatever else, make a statblock later I guess
