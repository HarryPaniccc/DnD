The **Deathwatch**, forged by [[Caede]] in the year 813 PF after [[Isilme Vanariel]]'s ascension to Dhampirism, is an order of [[The Raven Queen|Raven Queen]] followers, among its legion are those afflicted with undead or damage to the spirit who wish to continue their existance while atoning for their sins.
