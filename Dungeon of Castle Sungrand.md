Beneath [[Castle Sungrand]], connected to both the dungeons below the keep of the castle and the chapel to [[Ilmater]] is a much larger dungeon network below the castle, overrun with monsters and creatures, as well as the meeting place for [[Canon Valaris]] and his conspirators.

- Entrance in the keep dungeon
- Entrance behind a cracked wall at the temple
- Yellow Mold


# Level 1: Rooms 1 - 4
**The Darkest Dungeon Used**.
A small zone that acts as a transition from prison to true dungeon. Goes from prison space to the ruined keep.
>**Summaries**. Four **giant rats** guarding treasure.


## 1. Entry Hall
The first of the entrances to the dungeon is below the keep of castle sungrand. Down a dark staircase, on either side is a drop into a deep pit, as well as a slow moving, mostly blocked aquaduct, there sits a door that is locked and barred from the outside.
- **Iron Grate**. A grate leads down through a pipe to the sewers on level 2.
- **Damaged Doors**. Ancient wooden doors, hinges rotten in place at the end of the hall. Strength 15 to open.
- **Iron Bar Gate**. Locked (DC 12). Pathway used by Canon to descend into the dungeon.

## 2. Interrogation Chamber
**Stench**. Urine and feces is not often cleaned. A guard will often sit and watch.
- **Prisoner**. A disguised prisoner appears as a bugbear of the [[Khalar'hok]].
- **Light**. A lantern is hung on the left wall, giving the sitting guard some light.

## 3. Jail
**Smell**. Stagnant water and mold, muck pools around a blocked grate in the middle of the floor. **Cells**. Eight cells line the left and right wall of this chamber. All but the second and last on the left are locked (**DC 13 to lockpick**)
- **Iron Bar Gates**. Unlocked top, locked bottom, and leads onwards. Often used by Canon.
- **Cracked Wall**. The middle cell second from the bottom on the right has a wall with a large crack in it. Allows access to room 6.
- **Vermin**. Four **giant rats** attack if disturbed in the cracked wall cell. They snack on an old severed arm with a bracelet on it (**Silver set with moonstone, 60gp**).

## 4. Guard Office
**Rust and Dust**. Undisturbed for an incredibly long time. Dust is kicked up. **Nine** guard skeletons, died hiding. **Desk** with cobweb covered writing utensils and ruined guard ledgers. One open
- **Western Door**. Ancient doors leading to room [[#1. Entry Hall]]. Barred from this side, but the wooden bar crumbles like dough from rot.
- **Southern Door.** Same state as western door. Barred from this side. Rotted.
- **Open Guard Ledger**. "We die as cowards. Im sorry Amara. I love you."
- **Fearful Skeletons**. Nine skeletal remains of guards wearing long rusted chainmale. Spiders make homes from their chest cavities. Each has a pouch
- **Storage Room**. Used for guard supplies. Shelves line the walls. Ruined items stored in ruined chests, but one chest contains confiscated wealth to a total of **145 gp**, minted with the face of an old [[House Ralenroth]] king, as well as **62 tarnished sp** (can be transferred to hacksilver for 1/3 value).

# Level 1: Rooms 5 - 11
**Undisturbed Prison Ruins**.
This zone is untravelled, save for Canon's trips down the main vector of the dungeon. Clear marks of his passage are visible to those who look.
>**Summaries**: Wraith

## 5. Abandoned Prison Hall
**Coldness Radiates From Everywhere, cobwebs in every corner**. Places for shackles to be inserted along each wall. A sewer grate beneath them for waste. Chains hang from the ceiling.
- **Greatsword Pierced Corpse.** Chains rusted away with the ages, a corpse sags with a greatsword protruding from its chest. The greatsword is [[Resolve]]. Removing [[Resolve]] from the remains causes all lit flames in the chamber to go out and the skeleton to dissolve in a pool of liquid shadow and become a **Wraith** at the entrance to the chamber (flee mortals).


## 6. Mold Infested Hall
**Stench**. The smell of mold is incredibly intense in this hallway.
>[!infobox| right]
># Yellow Mold
>Touching the mold ejects a 10-foot cube of spores originating from the it. 15 CON save or **2D10 poison and become poisoned for 1 minute save ends at end of turn. While poisoned take 1d10 poison damage at the start of each turn.** Sunlight or any amount of fire damage destroys the mold.

- **Yellow Mold.** A patch grows out of a long dead skeleton and a series of dead rats out of the westmost cell blocking access to the corner, along the ground, stretching up the ceiling, along the wall. 
- **Golden Bracelet**. The skeleton clutches a golden bracelet in its hands. Red gem dragon eyes in the decoration. Value of 150 gp.

## 7. Ruined Stores
**Mold covers the room**. Ruined stores of grain. Remains of vermin are covered by the mold as well.
- **Healing Potions**. On the shelf, covered by mold, are three [[Potion of Healing|potions of healing]].

## 8. Opening to Sewers
**Crumbling Floor**. Leads to a water channel below. Entering causes the floor to give way below.


## 9. Dust and Bones
**Cavernous Once-Supply Room**.
**Bones**. The floor is littered with the remains of guards from eons ago. Something tore through these men. Spears are broken. Gargoyles and a spectator with a key in its mouth.
- **Pathway**. A path has been swept through the bones, leading to the door.
- **Balcony Overhead**. Watching down sit three gargoyles. Hiding around the corner is a spectator. **Mental communication**.
- **Infultrator Guards.** To ensure no one seeks this path, Canon has installed a **spectator** and three **gargoyles** to lie motionless and watch. The spectator has the key in its mouth.
- **Double Doors**. Bar is moved to the side. Locked **DC 15**. 

## 10. Ruined Tower Courtyard Antechamber
**Moss and roots.** The chamber is alive with mold and moss. Roots cover the natural ceiling. Vines encase the structures, except one lever.
- **Yellow Mold.** Growing over the graves and bones of skeletons. Patches in the corners of the rooms.
- **Levers**. Two sit in the center of the room, one wrapped in vines, the other, suspiciously clean, is a mimic.
- **Mold Skeletons**. 6 of the skeletons are inhabited by undead energies, and have also become home to a colony of mold. If the mimic attacks, the skeletons rise and join the action, looking to slay the PCs
