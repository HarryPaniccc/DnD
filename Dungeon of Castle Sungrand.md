Beneath [[Castle Sungrand]], connected to both the dungeons below the keep of the castle and the chapel to [[Ilmater]] is a much larger dungeon network below the castle, overrun with monsters and creatures, as well as the meeting place for [[Canon Valaris]] and his conspirators.

- Entrance in the keep dungeon
- Entrance behind a cracked wall at the temple
# Level 1
---
### Entry Hall
The first of the entrances to the dungeon is below the keep of castle sungrand. Down a dark staircase, on either side is a drop into a deep pit, there sits a door that is locked and barred from the outside.
- Hallway with **iron grate** on the floor leading to aquaduct on level 2
- Connected is a **storage room** picked clean and filled with rats picking at a corpse 
- An iron portcullis connects to the **torture chambers**. A DC 20 strength check can force it open. There is a lever on the wall next to it that can also be used to open it, but it is locked in place using a pad lock which can be opened with a DC 13 lockpicking check.
