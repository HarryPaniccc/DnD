Beneath [[Castle Sungrand]], connected to both the dungeons below the keep of the castle and the chapel to [[Ilmater]] is a much larger dungeon network below the castle, overrun with monsters and creatures, as well as the meeting place for [[Canon Valaris]] and his conspirators.

- Yellow Mold
# Level 1: Rooms 1 - x
A small zone that acts as a transition from prison to true dungeon. Goes from prison space to the ruined keep.
>**Summaries**. Two large and one medium **Ochre Jellies**. Large patch of **Yellow Mold**. **Wraith**. [[Resolve]].


## 1. Entry Hall
The first of the entrances to the dungeon is below the keep of castle sungrand. Down a dark staircase, on either side is a drop into a deep pit, as well as a slow moving, mostly blocked aquaduct, there sits a door that is locked and barred from the outside.
- **Iron Grate**. A grate leads down through a pipe to the sewers on level 2.
- **Damaged Doors**. Ancient wooden doors, hinges rotten in place at the end of the hall. Strength 15 to open.
- **Iron Bar Gate**. Locked (DC 12). Pathway used by Canon to descend into the dungeon.

## 2. Interrogation Chamber
**Stench**. Urine and feces is not often cleaned. A guard will often sit and watch.
- **Prisoner**. A disguised prisoner appears as a bugbear of the [[Khalar'hok]].
- **Light**. A lantern is hung on the left wall, giving the sitting guard some light.

## 3. Jail
**Smell**. Stagnant water and mold, muck pools around a blocked grate in the middle of the floor. **Cells**. Eight cells line the left and right wall of this chamber.
- **Iron Bar Gate**. Unlocked and leads onwards. Often used by Canon.
- **Cracked Wall**. The middle cell second from the bottom on the right has a wall with a large crack in it.

## 4. Guard Office
**Rust and Dust**. Undisturbed for an incredibly long time. Dust is kicked up. **Nine** guard skeletons, died hiding. **Desk** with cobweb covered writing utensils and ruined guard ledgers. One open
- **Western Door**. Ancient doors leading to room [[#1. Entry Hall]]. Barred from this side, but the wooden bar crumbles like dough from rot.
- **Southern Door.** Same state as western door. Barred from this side. Rotted.
- **Open Guard Ledger**. "We die as cowards. Im sorry Amara. I love you."
- **Fearful Skeletons**. Nine skeletal remains of guards wearing long rusted chainmale. Spiders make homes from their chest cavities.
- **Storage Room**. Used for guard supplies. Shelves line the walls. Ruined items stored in ruined chests, but one chest contains confiscated wealth to a total of 145 gp, minted with the face of an old [[House Ralenroth]] king, as well as 62 tarnished sp (can be transferred to hacksilver for 1/3 value).

## 5. Abandoned Prisoner Hall
**Cobwebs in every corner**. Places for shackles to be inserted along each wall. A sewer grate beneath them for waste. Chains hang from the ceiling.
- **Greatsword Pierced Corpse.** Chains rusted away with the ages, a corpse sags with a greatsword protruding from its chest. The greatsword is [[Resolve]]. Removing [[Resolve]] from the remains causes all lit flames in the chamber to go out and the skeleton to dissolve in a pool of liquid shadow and become a **Wraith** at the entrance to the chamber (flee mortals).

## 6. Mold Infested Hall
**Stench**. The smell of mold is incredibly intense in this hallway.
>[!infobox| right]
># Yellow Mold
>Touching the mold ejects a 10-foot cube of spores originating from the it. 15 CON save or **2D10 poison and become poisoned for 1 minute save ends at end of turn. While poisoned take 1d10 poison damage at the start of each turn.** Sunlight or any amount of fire damage destroys the mold.

- **Yellow Mold.** A patch grows out of a long dead skeleton and a series of dead rats out of the westmost cell blocking access to the corner, along the ground, stretching up the ceiling, along the wall.
