# Forging of [[Nightfall, the Hand that Feeds]]
## Process
The process to forge Nightfall requires mercury to be imbued with vampire blood and alchemically transmuted to a solid ingot. Then the ingot will need to be  imbued with immense magical force, as the Primordial Shard is then hammered into the metal. This task cannot be done by a machine, there needs to be a will behind it or the shard won't stick. It'll just devour the power and remain as it is.

Then, before it has a moment to consolidate itself, the weapon must be shaped. Immense heat and might and skill, as all great blades require.

The sheath requires soft obsidian on the interior. Kanan is baffled to think such a substance exists, but Grelchar tells him it's real and that he can get it here. The dwarves have been known to tan leather from stone before, so Kanan just goes with it.

The guard will be crafted of carved gold with a gradient purity, becoming more impure towards the hilt to channel the powers of the weapon toward the will of the wielder.

The most important part of the process is the lock and chain. It will need to be a holy implement, forged of platinum and each rung quenched in holy oils so that the monstrous will and power of the weapon can be subdued.

## Minigame:
Alchemy check to get the mercury: DC 18
Strength check to force the shard into the ingot: DC 40 - Twice spell levels forced into the ingot. Can reattempt
Constitution check to forge the blade into the correct shape: DC 22. Can reattempt.
