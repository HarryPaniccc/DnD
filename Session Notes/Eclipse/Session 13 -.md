## Post Mortem


## Recap
**Much has happened** these past months, too much to tell now, but here is what is most important.
- Our group of unlikely heroes managed to fight their way out of the besieged city of [[Nethendell]], through the forest known as [[Arden's Vale]], defeat the monsters holding [[Vraath Keep]], and eventually make their way to the last bastion in the Nethendell Valley, [[Castle Sungrand]].
- They set down a few roots in the keep, seeing to their gear and lodgings, as well as buying drinks for a large contingent of soldiers, and eventually met with the lord of the fortress, [[Lord Margit Tormon|Margit Tormon]] and his military advisor, a paladin by the name of [[Canon Valaris]].
- Their request to aid the refugees, however, was denied. Advice from Canon prevents the defensive perimeter around the castle from expanding, so for the time being, the party continued seeing to their gear. 
- During their day, the group caught wind of the sudden disappearance of a hero of the town, [[Kalir Windguard]], and her friend and ally, the head cleric of the Sungrand chapel, [[Gothrend Grinda]]. [[Cened]] arranged with an acolyte by the name of [[Belle the Acolyte|Belle]] to investigate into Gothrend's sudden disappearance.
- At midnight that evening, the group was attacked in the [[Borderlands Inn]] by a group of wraiths and their shadow minions. Though they managed to fight the undead off, the inn was brought to ruin, and many of the patrons and staff were brutally slain. [[Lord Margit Tormon|Margit Tormon]], realizing the potential importance of your group, offered for you to lodge within the inner keep of Castle Sungrand for the time being, to provide protection, more likely for the people under his care, than for you.
