## Recap
**Much has happened** these past months, too much to tell now, but here is what is most important.
- Having convinced lord Tormon to let you explore the bowels of the dungeon of [[Castle Sungrand]], you delved into an ancient prison, long abandoned by the occupants of the castle.
- Knowing that the subterranean structure stretches possibly under the entirety of the castle, and maybe further, you progressed south, hoping to find your way to the tomb below the chapel
- Making your way through the dungeon slowly and carefully, you walk past grates and crumbling floors, leading to a sewer or aquaduct system below, and eventually made your way into a large structure filled with the destroyed bones of long-dead soldiers.
- While rummaging through the remains, your minds were probed by an external presence, and a massive, single-eyed head, with four eyestalks protruding from it, descended upon you, attacking.
