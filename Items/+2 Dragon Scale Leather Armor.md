Dragon scale leather armour is forged using the scales of an adult dragon. It has variants depending on the type of dragon used.



>[!infobox|wfull]
> # +2 Red Dragon Scale Leather Armour
> ***Armour (Supple Leather), Very Rare, Requires Attunement***
> 
> | |
> |-|
> This crimson armour consists of bracers and a breastplate crafted from the hide of an adult red dragon. In areas where flexibility can be sacrificed there is mail of iron hard scales. You have a +2 bonus to AC and resistance to fire damage while you wear this armour.
