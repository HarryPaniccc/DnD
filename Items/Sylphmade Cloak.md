># Sylphmade Cloak
> ***Wondrous Item (Cloak), Very Rare, Requires Attunement***
> 
> | |
> |-|
> This item is a bright scarf made by the spirits of of the elemental plane of air. It is woven of cloudsilk, and can be worked into a variety of shapes ranging from cloaks to cape to scarf. A large amount of cloudlike embroidery adorns the edges of the Cloak. While wearing the cloak, you gain the following benefits.
>- You have a flying speed of 50 feet and can hover.
>- If you aren't wearing armour, your base Armor Class is 14 + your Dexterity modifier.
>- You can cast the *Freedom of Movement* and *Gust of Wind* spells on yourself once per day each, recharging at dawn (Spell save DC of 16). The Cloak maintains concentration for you.
