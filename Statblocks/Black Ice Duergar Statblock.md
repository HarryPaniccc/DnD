> # Traits
> **Legendary Resistances (3/Day).** 
> \
> **Duergar Resilience**. The duergar has advantage on saving throws against poison, spells, and illusions, as well as to resist being charmed or paralyzed.
> \
> **Sunlight Sensitivity**. While in sunlight, the duergar has disadvantage on attack rolls, as well as on Wisdom (Perception) checks that rely on sight.

### Actions
**Enlarge (1/rest).** Magically increase in size including everything it is wearing or carrying. While enlarged, it is Large, doubles damage dice on strength-based weapon attacks, and makes Strength checks and saves with advantage.

**Bite.** +11 to hit, 10ft reach, *Hit:* 2d10 + 6 piercing plus 1d8 cold

**Invisibility (1/rest).** Becomes invisible and has to concentrate on it until it uses an attack, uses Enlarge, or casts a spell. Lasts for 1 hour otherwise.

**Cold Breath (Recharge 5-6).** 60-foot cone, DC 19 CON save, 12d8 cold damage, half on save.
