> # Traits
> **Mold Colony.** The animated remains are a home for a colony of *Yellow Mold*. Once per turn, when the skeleton takes damage, all creatures within 5ft. of the skeleton suffer (1d4) poison damage.
>When the dies it leaves behind a 5ft. square patch of *Yellow Mold* if its body was not totally destroyed and it was not slain with fire or radiant damage.

### Actions
**Shortsword.** +4 to hit, 5ft. reach, *Hit:* (1d6 + 2 piercing) damage.

**Shortbow.** +4 to hit, range 80/320 ft. *Hit:* (1d6 + 2 piercing damage).

**Poison Spores (Recharge 6).** The mold colony ejects a 10-foot cube of spores originating from it. 15 CON save or 2d10 poison damage and become poisoned for 1 minute (save ends at end of turn). While poisoned this way, take 1d10 poison damage at the start of each of your turns.
