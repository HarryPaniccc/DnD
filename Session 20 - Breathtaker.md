## Recap
**Much has happened** these past days, too much to tell now, but here is what is most important.
- Having investigated the strange goings on in the sewers below [[Castle Sungrand]], the party uncovered monsters and infultrators, an oni by the name of [[Malphur]] disguising himself as the paladin [[Canon Valaris]], the military advisor to lord [[Lord Margit Tormon|Margit Tormon]], the party has curried favour with the army of the castle, and rescued the real Canon from his terrible torture in the prison below.
- Now, as inventory is taken and Canon begins to assess the status of the standing army, you were advised to begin projecting your power out from the safety of the perimeter around castle sungrand.
- At the breakfast you shared with lord Tormon and the recovering Canon, information was shared with you about the goings on around the region surrounding [[Castle Sungrand]]. Strange goings-on in the forest, [[Vraath Keep]] being used as a staging ground for the army, slavers in [[Blackhand Tower]], undead in the marshes east of the castle, and the Enemy consolidating power around the Western Watchtower. 
- Deciding to investigate the strange occurances in the forest, you. headed north, running into a small family of refugees and curing some amount of illness, before plunging into the forest.
- As the sounds of animal life die down and a dread fog slowly rolled in, you began to be hunted by the massive reptilian form of a green dragon, toying with his food. [[Palax]] offered a handful of silver to try to bribe the dragon. Kindly, the Dragon accepted the two silver offered, giving you two minutes of time to run, as he took to the skies, preparing a hunt.
