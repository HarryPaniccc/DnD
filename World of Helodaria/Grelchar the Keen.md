**Grelchar the Keen** is the master forger of the [[Steinwalde Heart-Kiln]].
# Description
---
Grelchar has grey hair and a bald head. He often wears leathers and clothes that cover his arms to protect from the heat. His skin is leathery from the heat of the heart kiln. His voice is gruff with no accent, despite speaking Dwarvish and Sirkonian.
