# Class: Monk
### Level 1 - start
**HP**: 8 + 4d8 + 5 x CON = 13 + 8 + 3 + 5 + 6 = 35
**Hit Dice**: 5d8

**Prof**: simple weapons, shortswords, Cooks utensils
**Saves**: Strength and Dexterity
**Skills**: Stealth and Acrobatics

**Starting Equipment**: 
- Light hammer
- Explorer's Pack
- 10 darts

**Unarmored Defense**: AC = 10 + Dex + Wis

**Martial Arts**: Use Dex and can bonus action punch when you attack as an action
### Level 2 - Ki
One Ki per level repletish all during short rest

**Key Ki Features**
- Flurry of Blows
- Patient Defence
- Step of the Wind

**Unarmored Movement**: +10ft.

### Level 3 - Sublcass  + Deflect Missiles
**Deflect Missiles**: Reaction to minus 1d10 + DEX + monk level and can send it back (read the feature)

#### **Way of Mercy**
- **Proficiency** Insight and Medicine, and a herbalism kit
- **Hand of Healing**: Action touch to heal martial arts die + WIS. can replace a FOB punch for this instead (for free)
- **Hand of Harm**: 1/turn to spend a ki point to add martial arts + WIS mod damage

### Level 4 - ASI/Feats + Slow Fall
5 x Monk level reduce damage

Mobile Feat
- +10ft
- Dashing = no difficult terrain
- Making melee = disabling opportunity attacks

### Level 5 - Extra Attack and Stunning Strike
**Extra Attack**: Attack twice on your turn
**Stunning Strike**: Force a save vs stun
