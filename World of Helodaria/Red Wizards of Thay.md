The [[Red Wizards of Thay]] are a religion of powerful wizards. Their members tattoo their shaven heads and faces with runes demarking their devotion and power within the religious order. They scheme to maintain as much control over [[Desseran]] and the rest of [[Eranulaid]], and the schemes of their individual members often extend beyond that.

# Description
# History
Once the magocratic rulers of [[Thay]] before the empire fell near the end of [[The Fall]]. They grew to power as powerful slavers, necromancers, and demonologists, but eventually after their empire fell to ruin under its own weight, and after the resurgence, they were reduced to a fraction of their previous reach. Now, they are a faction of the ruling class of [[Desseran]].
