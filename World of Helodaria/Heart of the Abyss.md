The [[Heart of the Abyss]] is the artifact that acts as the single unifying element to the chaos of the [[Abyss]]. During [[The Fall]], the [[Demons|Demon Lord]] [[Eltab]] gave his heart as a physical vessel for the [[Seed of the Abyss]] so that a divine unifying messiah for the abyss could be created. The heart fused with [[King Sirkon|Sirkon]], and he became the Demon King, unifying the forces of demon kind against [[Desseran]].

The heart was ripped out of his chest near the end of the fall by the [[Unnamed Avatar of the Bahamut]], and has since been sealed in the [[Tomb of the God Emperor]], guarded by [[Imvaernarhro|Inferno]] in [[Anaroth the Sun Mountains]].
