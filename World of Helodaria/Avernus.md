**Avernus** is the first layer of the [[Nine Hells]], and the entrypoint to the plane. It is ruled by the archdevil [[Zariel]]. It is a blasted hellscape home to devils and the primary battleground of the [[Blood War]]. Avernus is also home, and prison, to the dragon god [[Tiamat]].
