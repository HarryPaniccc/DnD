[[The Fall]] was not the worst calamity the world has ever seen. Perhaps it is one of the biggest losses of life, but the laws of the universe remained unharmed by its occurrances... mostly. The [[Dawn War]], preceding the [[Age of Ascension]] by uncountable millenia, left the world broken in ways that none could fathom. The magic and understanding of the universe, the cities and monuments, and whatever else existed during your father's father's childhood is gone. We can barely forge bronze. The gods leave proverbial trails of blood across a sky that was once theirs.

This is the [[Age of Wounds]]
