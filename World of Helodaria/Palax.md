# Creation
## Race: Red Dragonborn
- ASI - +2STR, +1 Cha
- 30ft speed
- 15 ft. cone, fire damage, Dex save (DC = 8 + CON + Prof), at lvl6 3d6
- Resistant to fire damage
- Lang: Common + Draconic

## Class: Barb 6
**Level 1**
- d12 hit die
- HP = 12 + 6CON + 5D12 + 2lvl

**Prof**
- light, medium, shields (armour)
- Simple and Martial Weapons
- STR and CON saves
- Athletics and Survival

#### Rage
- Bonus action for angry
- Adv on str checks and saves, +2 to damage rolls
- Resets on long rests

#### Unarmored Defence
AC = 10 + DEX + CON

#### Danger Sense
- Advantage on DEX saves on things you can see

#### Reckless Attack
- Get advantage on all melee attacks but get advantage against you too

### Path of the Zealot
- Divine Fury (first hit does more damage = 1d6 + half level)
- Warrior of the Gods (free resurrection spells yippee)

### Tough Feat
Add 2x level to HP when you get it, and add 2 per levelup
### Extra Attack
### Fast Movement
10ft. movement while not in heavy armor
### Fanatical Focus
While raging, reroll failed save and must use new roll. 1/rage
