# Description
Theord Homestead is a small logging village named after its founder, [[Theord the Outlander]], sitting in the western plateaus of the [[Maghlas Mountains]] north of the [[Faults of North Estravia]]. It is 2 days south of [[Steinwalde]].

It consists of 6 houses and a series of animal pens where the goats are kept, and [[Theord the Outlander]] and [[Daurgi the Herdsman]] live there.
# History
[[Theord the Outlander]] founded the village circa 795 PF, when he built his home there. He has been wealthy by logger's standards, and a number of shepards and herdsmen have moved around him from other nearby villages.
