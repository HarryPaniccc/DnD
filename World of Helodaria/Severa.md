**Lady Severa** is one of the leaders of the [[Red Wizards of Thay]] and works as one of their chief agents, with interests centred on [[Desseran]], but extending to the entirety of [[Eranulaid]].

She has a captive vampire lord who she magically uses to control seven powerful vampire spawn.
