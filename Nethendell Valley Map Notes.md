# Hex Key
#### 11 10
Deep in the forest lay the ruins of an elven town. Beautifully wrought stone statues, centred around a courtyard act as the centrepiece of the now overgrown ruins. Here, the corpses of some of the [[Khalar'hok]]'s victims have been left to stew.

#### 11 12
[[Vraath Keep]] stits atop a hill in this forested hex. Pathways criss cross surrounding the keep, from which you can see [[Castle Sungrand]] clearly in the fields beyond [[Arden's Vale]].
