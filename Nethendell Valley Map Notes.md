# Random Encounters
> Move, roll, describe, react. That is the flow of random overland encounters.

Steps to determining the encounter
1. Roll on encounter by terrain classification
2. Roll on the associated table for a specific terrain type to determine what encounter you will get.
3. Roll on any subsequent tables to determine the nature of the encounter.

**Cache**: Wealth, hidden from view, an abandoned carriage of mining supplies, or pit hidden by leaves containing ammunition or weapons. Someone stored something in this location, out of sight. **Perception 15** to keep an eye on the lay of the land will reveal a path or a clue to the cache, but not the cache itself.

**Food**: A source of food is visible here. Whether that be a fat flock of fowl, a lucky orchard of apples, or an abandoned carriage with some food provisions remaining. 1d4 rations worth of food can be claimed from this source.

**Fountain**: A source of water that does not impede movement, but does provide clean drinking water.

**Hidden Settlement**: A forward base, a small elven village, or kobold lair filled with honey or whatever else the kobolds have looted.

**Monster Carcass**: The deceased remains of a monster from the monster table appropriate to the region. Roll on the monster encounter table.

**Ore**: Jutting from a ravine, a rocky outcrop, the side of a road, or a river, there is a natural ore of some kind that can either be mined now (like a singular gem) or returned to for future profits.

# Hex Key

#### 09 14 | Blackhand Fortress
Visible from two hexes away, [[Blackhand Fortress]] is another decrepit structure of stone that has been occupied by the [[Goldrend Bandits]]. They operate their slaving operation from this location. It is well defended.

#### 11 10 | Breathtaker's Lair
**Thick Forest**
This region is coated in a thick fog. This is the main hunting ground of the young green [[Dragons of Helodaria|Dragon]] [[Breathtaker]]. His lair is a decrepit druid's grove that has been built on the elven mausoleum for the dead who fought in this area during [[The Fall]].

Deep in the forest lay the ruins of an elven town. Beautifully wrought stone statues, centred around a courtyard act as the centrepiece of the now overgrown ruins. Here, the corpses of some of the [[Breathtaker]]'s victims have been left to stew.

#### 11 12 | Vraath Keep
**Thick Forest**
[[Vraath Keep]] stits atop a hill in this forested hex. Pathways criss cross surrounding the keep, from which you can see [[Castle Sungrand]] clearly in the fields beyond [[Arden's Vale]].

#### 12 11 | Soldier's Camp
**Forest**
Near the entrance to the forest in this region sits a soldier's camp, a guard base for [[Castle Sungrand]] soldiers. A large number of tents, supplies, and maps are laid out. From here, rangings into the forest to resist the invasion are sent out.
