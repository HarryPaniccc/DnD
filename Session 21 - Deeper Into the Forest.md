## Recap
**Much has happened** these past weeks, too much to tell now, but here is what is most important.
- While traversing the forest north of [[Castle Sungrand]], [[Arden's Vale]], our group of adventurers were in the process of scouting out rumours that soldiers had been disappearing in the forest, as well as just exploring the dense woods.
- As they plunged deeper into the forest, a dense fog surrounded them, cutting off their view of their surroundings even more than the already dense foliage was, and all sounds of animals disappeared. Eventually, the group realized that they were being hunted by a green dragon.
- In an insulting attempt to parlay with the monster, they offered him two silver pieces, and in exchange, he gave them two minutes of time. Attempts to lead him on a false trail failed, and the group fled towards [[Vraath Keep]].
- Preparing an ambush, the group themselves were attacked by the then-invisible dragon, only [[Ferro]] noticing the beast's movement through the underbrush. What followed was a terrible melee, but at the end of it, due to the sudden arrival of [[Swift]] and [[Togwae]], the heroes managed to slay the fleeing dragon, whose body now lies smoldering on the forest floor.
