The [[Seed of the Abyss]] is an incredibly powerful artefact created in an attempt to corrupt the [[Primordials]]. The primordials that were corrupted became the [[Obyriths]], an eldritch race of [[Demons]] predating mortal existence (in normal dnd lore). The Seed is the source of the [[Abyss]], and was used by [[Dagon]] to corrupt [[Tharizdun]].

During [[The Fall]], the heart of the imprisoned [[Demons|Demon]] lord [[Eltab]] was used as a physical vessel for the seed, and the two were fused into the artifact, the [[Heart of the Abyss]].
